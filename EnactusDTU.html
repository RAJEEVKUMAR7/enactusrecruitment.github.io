WHO ARE WE?

OUR PROJECTS

PROJECT MILAN

Milan revolutionizes the traditional farming industry by the introduction of aquaponics as it’s core system. Our work has resulted in a 90% reduction in water consumption to a whopping decrease of about 57% in plastic usage. Milan is the future of organic farming and an answer to the plights of every farmer.

PROJECT UNNATI

Project Unnati has done wonders in slums across Delhi-NCR by imparting digital literacy, opening up job avenues, and closing the gap between them and technology. Not only has it developed more than 10 fully functional cafes around Delhi, but it has also created a healthy livelihood of 18,000 per month for every young entrepreneur. Project Unnati has powered more than 1,50,000 rural households and impacted more than 2,50,000 lives.

PROJECT SWAJAL

Project Swajal is Enactus DTU’s answer to one of the most important sustainable development goals set by the UN - clean drinking and sanitation. It was one of the frontrunners for Enactus’ World Water Race and has impacted more than 25,000 lives with clean water and a life full of longevity.

PROJECT RANGMANCH

India is home to hundreds of cultural heritages which are fading away with the passage of time. Project Rangmanch focuses on giving life to these intangible art forms and empower the artisans. In the pilot program, we worked with the puppeteers which resided in backward areas of Delhi, NCR, India.
